# Product Overview

**Section:** 10 Product

[Previous: Human Product Overview](./02-human-product-overview.md) | [Index](./01-index.md) | [Next: Platform Features](./05-platform-features.md)

openCRM is a lightweight CRM platform for small teams and individuals who need more flexibility than a simple contact manager but less complexity than a large enterprise CRM.

## Product Goal

The product aims to combine:

- CRM essentials like contacts, companies, opportunities, and cases
- runtime customization through custom objects and fields
- role-aware apps and dashboards
- practical access control through ownership, queues, groups, and sharing

The key business idea is that users should be able to shape the system around their workflow without changing the database schema every time a new business entity is needed.

## What Makes It Different

openCRM is not just a fixed CRM. It is also a small platform.

That means the system supports:

- standard CRM modules
- user-defined business objects
- admin-built apps for different teams
- configurable list views and dashboards
- configurable business rules such as assignment rules and sharing rules
