# openCRM Manual

## 08. Record Pages

### Record pages

Record pages control what the user sees when opening a record. They decide which fields appear in highlights, which sections exist, how many columns a section uses, and what stays visible in the detail experience.

- **Highlights**: The highlight area places the most important fields directly under the record name for quick scanning.
- **Sections**: Sections group fields into readable blocks and can be arranged into single-column or multi-column layouts.

![Record page builder](images/admin-record-page-builder.png)

*The record page builder lets an administrator drag fields into highlights and sections, shape the page layout, and control how records appear in the standard app.*
